  • IOTEX M100

    As part of our research and development work at Irro'keys, we launched the IOTEX project, The IOT Extra, is the new generation of hardware solutions, for the supervision and diagnosis of a production chain through an intelligent module based on acquisition and processing real-time images. The use of model-free and data-based diagnostic and fault detection methods, in the context of so-called complex systems, is now becoming an interesting and often necessary alternative to conventional methods. We position ourselves at Irro'keys in this perspective and research & development area and concerning the integration of intelligent modules in the industrial sector..
