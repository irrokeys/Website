Our story

irro'keys was created by two former colleagues, a research professor and a research engineer. With significant experience in the fields of engineering and a solid theoretical and academic background, the two founders saw irro'keys as a research & development startup aspiring to creation and continuous innovation in the fields of high tech technologies.

irro'keys is also designed to meet a real need in industry and compagnies. In the era of digitization and Industry 4.0, the demands and expectations in terms of productivity, performance, quality, portability, accessibility and modularity are increasingly important. One of the priorities for irro'keys is therefore the support and monitoring of companies in this adventure of convergence towards this industry of the future.
